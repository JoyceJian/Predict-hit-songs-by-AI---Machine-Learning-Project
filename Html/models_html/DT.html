Decision Tree


For an initial identification of clusters in the data, we used the Decision Tree to creates a series of decision boundaries on the training set. Each boundary splits the data into two clusters (within the current cluster) at a value of a feature that minimizes the Gini loss.


This model gives a test accuracy of 0.3079.
